Beyond The Magic Hour







The Start of a Hobby

As a child, the end of classes in March was something I looked forward to. Why? It was the start of my summer vacation, a time to relax, have fun, and enjoy an out of town trip with the family. It was during one of these out of town trips that I discovered the beauty of sunsets. And not just sunsets. There is a term called the "Magic Hour" or the "Golden Hour" which is the period just before sunrise or just after sunset. People love to take photos of the setting sun. I love it too. But if you stick around a little while longer, you'll find yourself surrounded by the beauty of the night sky. The dusk makes me anticipate the mesmerizing mix of colors. And every night is a different play on these colors. I am in awe of its beauty, the feeling of calmness it gives me, and the sense of gratitude for a day's end.

Make It Yours

This is a hobby that is free, easy to do, and can be done anywhere you are. To check the sunset time in your area, click here. I'm sure there are other sites that give out this information. This is my go-to site. Prepare your (I use my ), and drive to the area where you think you'll have a perfect view to capture that moment. Keep in mind that you'll have to wait till after sundown to experience the beauty of the "Magic Hour" and beyond. The night sky is a huge canvas of playful colors. Just wait and see the magic that happens.
